A Thought Experiment

People Using AI Fall Into
3 Groups

Which one are you? Most people don't even realize which group they're in — until they do.

Group 1

The Copy-Paster

Uses AI outputs without ever questioning them.

  • ❌ No Verification
  • ❌ Blind Trust
  • ❌ Minimal Prompting
Group 2

The AI User

Uses AI frequently — but only as a convenience tool.

  • 〰️ Basic Prompting
  • 〰️ Occasional Verification
  • 〰️ Limited AI Understanding
Group 3

The AI-Literate User

Understands how AI works — and exactly where it fails.

  • ✅ Critical Thinking
  • ✅ Strategic Prompting
  • ✅ Cross-Checking

Topics We Cover

What You'll Learn

01

AI Ethics

Explore bias, fairness, privacy, and the moral questions that come with building and using powerful AI systems.

02

General AI

Understand how machine learning, large language models, and neural networks actually work — in plain language.

03

AI in Health

Discover how AI is transforming diagnostics, drug discovery, mental health support, and patient care.

04

AI in Finance

Learn how AI is reshaping personal finance, investing, fraud detection, and financial literacy for everyone.

05

Prompt Engineering

Get better results from AI tools by learning how to ask the right questions and guide outputs strategically.

06

Critical Thinking

Build the habits to evaluate AI-generated content, spot misinformation, and stay skeptical in the right ways.
